An app/website for ensembles that allows them to communicate, practice, learn music, and more!
Features:
Different account types:
- Director (can be multiple per ensemble)
- Student/member (with sub types: section leader, drum major, regular, ect...)
- Solo/pro

Practice logging that directors can see per member or overall ensemble.
Switching between ensembles if you are in multiple.
Uploading/adding pieces for yourself (students can only do that, solo does that) or for the ensemble (directors can do that or do for themselves).
Chat where members can communicate in their sections or however the director sets it up (same type of stuff as in announcements but as a regular chat, like in this github repo (a chat app i am working on with lovable): https://github.com/fallturtle/chathub-pro).
Practice scheduling.
Directors can send out announcements to an entire ensemble, section leaders to their section, drum majors to everyone or to just section leaders, director to just section leaders, ect...
Practice tools:
- Rhythm counter
- Fingering charts
- Smart tuner (tells you what to do to get into tune based on ur instrument)
- Metronome

Practicing you music with a follow line and metronome, also able to check what a note is and what a fingering is.
Directors can edit music.
Per user settings:
- Profile stuff
- Account type (can be different for different ensembles btw)
- Instrument
- Deleting account
- Appearence
- Change email
- Change password
- Other preferences

Different layouts for mobile (bottom bar) vs desktop (side bar)
Dashboard where user can see stuff like recent pieces, announcements for the current ensemble they have selected, changing ensemble, number of pieces, practice time, ect...
